20 Years of Faith in Action

Founded in 2004 in Wellington, Western Cape, Victorious Pilgrim Ministries exists to obey the Great Commission. Under the leadership of Chairman Ashwell W. Beaton and Welfare Director Wendy Beaton, we combine evangelism with practical mercy ministry.

"Where God guides, He will provide. If it is God’s will, it becomes His bill."

We worship and serve alongside local SDA congregations while reaching the wider community with the love of Jesus — through literature, health education, food parcels, clothing and much more.

"We would like to thank each individual for their sacrifice, time, talent, expertise and financial support. You have made a valuable contribution in spreading the gospel throughout South Africa and beyond."

— Ashwell W. Beaton, VPM Chairman
