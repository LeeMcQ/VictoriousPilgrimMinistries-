Who We Are

Faith in Action Since 2004

REG. NO. 2004/028180/08 · WELLINGTON, WESTERN CAPE

Founded in 2004 in Wellington, Western Cape, Victorious Pilgrim Ministries exists to obey the Great Commission. Under the leadership of Chairman Ashwell W. Beaton and Welfare Director Wendy Beaton, VPM combines bold evangelism with practical mercy ministry.

We worship and serve alongside local SDA congregations while reaching the wider community with the love of Jesus — through literature, health education, food parcels, clothing and much more. Over two decades, our team has touched thousands of lives across 23 towns in South Africa.

"Where God guides, He will provide. If it is God's will, it becomes His bill."

— Ashwell W. Beaton, VPM Chairman

What We Do

Two Arms · One Mission

Everything we do flows from a single calling: to be the hands and feet of Jesus to those in need.

Evangelism Ministry

  • 7,109 books & 280 Bibles distributed across South Africa
  • 42 house visits and 47 personal Bible studies conducted
  • 210 gospel tracts, 160 DVDs and 36 counselling sessions
  • Preaching, Sabbath School leadership and youth evangelism
  • Open-air services in schools, halls and public spaces
  • Outreach in 23 towns: Oudtshoorn, Paarl, Vredendal, Montagu & more

Welfare & Health Ministry

  • 8,500+ clothing items donated to those in need
  • 210 food parcels, 1,540 plates served & 130L of soup distributed
  • Daily sandwiches, pies, muffins, fresh fruit & vegetable parcels
  • BMI testing, blood pressure monitoring & health education talks
  • Haircuts, massages and personal hygiene pack distribution
  • Medical referrals and ongoing welfare support for families
